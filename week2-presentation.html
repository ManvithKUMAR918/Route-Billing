Week 1 Recap

Problem Statement & Objectives

🎯 Problem

Transport route assignments, fee collection, and parent communication at FirstCry Intellitots are managed manually — causing missed payments, billing errors, and zero visibility into transport costs.

📋 Objectives

• Digitize route assignments & fee billing
• Automate dues detection & reminders
• Track transport expenses & profitability
• AI-powered insights for defaulter prediction
• Full lifecycle: enquiry → admission → exit

Literature Survey

What the Research Says

📉 Revenue Leakage

Manual tracking causes 10–15% revenue loss from missed collections and unrecorded route changes.

— Schoolites.com (2024)

⚡ Digital Payments

Automated billing systems significantly reduce processing time and minimize manual errors.

— Nsamwa et al., SCIRP (2025)

🤖 AI in Transport

AI-driven optimization reduces fuel costs by 15–20% with predictive payment analytics.

— eLearning Industry (2025)

Literature Survey (Cont.)

Challenges & Opportunities

💬 Parent Communication

Real-time fee & bus updates lead to higher on-time payment rates and parent satisfaction.

— EduSys, EdTech Review (2024–25)

🇮🇳 Indian Context

Only 30–40% of preschools have fully digitized fee management. Legacy inertia remains a barrier.

— Indian Express (2024–25)

🔗 Integrated ERP

Standalone tools create operational silos. Integrated systems improve forecasting & reduce admin burden.

— NASSCOM / EDUCAUSE (2023–25)

Key Takeaway: No single existing tool addresses all these needs for Indian preschools.

Existing System Analysis

What Already Exists

🛰️ Edsys TrackSchoolBus

GPS tracking, RFID attendance, route optimization for K-12 schools in India & Middle East.

No Fee Billing No Enquiry Mgmt Hardware Dependent

🌟 Brightwheel

All-in-one preschool management — billing, daily reports, messaging. US market only.

No Transport Module US-Centric / No UPI No Exit Workflow
Existing System Analysis (Cont.)

The Gap No One Fills

💳 MySchoolBucks

US school payment portal for cafeteria & fees. Per-transaction fee of $3.25.

US-Only Meal-Focused No Transport No Communication

🔍 Core Gap

Edsys tracks buses but doesn't bill.
Brightwheel manages preschools but ignores transport.
MySchoolBucks collects payments but is US-only.

No tool combines all these for Indian preschools.
