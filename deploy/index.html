Week 1 Recap

Problem Statement & Objectives

Setting context — what we identified and where we're headed.

🎯

Problem Statement

FirstCry Intellitots manages transport for multiple routes across its preschool centre in Pune. Currently, route assignments, fee collection, payment tracking, and parent communication are handled manually via registers and spreadsheets — leading to missed payments, billing errors, and zero visibility into transport profitability.

📋

Objectives

  • Digitize transport route assignments & fee billing
  • Automate payment tracking, dues detection & reminders
  • Track transport expenses for profitability analysis
  • Provide AI-powered insights & defaulter prediction
  • Manage full student lifecycle: enquiry → exit
  • Enable automated parent messaging (WhatsApp/SMS)
Literature Survey

What the Research Says

Key findings from journals, industry reports, and credible sources.

📉

Revenue Leakage in Manual Systems

Schools using manual transport fee tracking report 10–15% revenue leakage from missed collections, unrecorded route changes, and poor reconciliation.

— Schoolites.com, Industry Report (2024)

⚡

Digital Payments Cut Processing Time

Systems linking transport attendance to automated billing significantly reduce processing time and minimize manual errors in fee calculation.

— Nsamwa et al., SCIRP (2025)

🤖

AI in School Transport

AI-driven route optimization reduces fuel costs by 15–20%. Predictive analytics enables payment pattern detection and dynamic operational adjustments.

— eLearning Industry (2025)

Literature Survey (Cont.)

Landscape & Challenges

The Indian preschool context and the case for integrated systems.

💬

Parent Communication Gap

Parents receiving real-time updates on bus status and fee reminders show significantly higher satisfaction and on-time payment rates. Informal WhatsApp groups are being replaced by centralized platforms.

— Pathshala ERP, EduSys, EdTech Review (2024–25)

🇮🇳

Indian Digitization Challenges

Only 30–40% of Indian preschools have fully digitized fee management. State regulations (e.g., Maharashtra capping bus fares), legacy inertia, and "partial adoption" remain key barriers.

— Indian Express, Axoneura.in (2024–25)

🔗

Integrated ERP as Strategic Necessity

Standalone tools create operational silos. Schools with integrated systems (tuition + transport + communication in one ledger) report better financial forecasting and reduced admin burden.

— NASSCOM / EDUCAUSE / Frontiers in Education (2023–25)

Key Synthesis: The research consistently shows that manual transport fee management causes measurable revenue loss, and that integrated, AI-enhanced systems with strong parent communication are the proven path forward — yet no single existing tool addresses all these needs for Indian preschools.

Existing System Analysis

What Already Exists

Three tools studied — each strong in one area, but none covers the full picture.

🛰️

Edsys TrackSchoolBus

End-to-end transport tracking with real-time GPS, RFID-based attendance, route optimization, and parent alerts. Used by K-12 schools in India & Middle East.

No Fee Billing No Enquiry Mgmt Hardware Dependent K-12 Only No AI Insights
🌟

Brightwheel

All-in-one preschool management — billing, enrollment, daily activity reports, parent messaging, attendance. Top-rated for US childcare centres.

No Transport Module US-Centric No UPI / INR No Exit Workflow No Expense Tracking
Existing System Analysis (Cont.)

The Payment-Only Gap

Payment platforms exist, but they don't address transport or Indian preschool needs.

💳

MySchoolBucks

Online school payment platform for cafeteria payments, fee collection, and activity registrations. Used by US K-12 districts. Per-transaction fee of $3.25–$3.50.

US-Only Meal-Focused No Transport Features No Communication High Transaction Fees No Indian Payments

🔍 The Core Gap

Edsys tracks buses but doesn't bill. Brightwheel manages preschools but ignores transport. MySchoolBucks collects payments but has no Indian presence.

No existing tool combines: transport route management + transport-specific fee billing + Indian localization + AI insights + full student lifecycle management — in a single platform.
